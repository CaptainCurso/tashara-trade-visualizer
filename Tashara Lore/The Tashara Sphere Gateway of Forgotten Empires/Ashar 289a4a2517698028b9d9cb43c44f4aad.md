# Ashar

Owner: Nicholas McDowell

Ashar is a cold, rust-red desert world with a thin but breathable atmosphere, its surface dominated by ancient evaporated seabeds, dry riverbeds, and vast dust-bowl basins divided by mountain ranges—a Mars-like planet where nights can be lethally frigid and days rarely rise above comfortable temperatures. The planet is inhabited by the **Asharen**, an insectoid species of six-limbed beings standing four feet tall with chitinous exoskeletons in shades of red to orange-brown, compound eyes adapted for infrared vision, and the remarkable ability to enter suspended animation during extreme cold. The Asharen evolved in underground caverns where geothermal heat provided survival, and they've built vast subterranean cities through sophisticated excavation techniques, their society organized around depth-clans where deeper territories mean higher status due to warmth and comfort. What makes the Asharen unique is their obsession with their sister world Karesh, which hangs massive in their sky—they believe Karesh is the afterlife where souls travel after death, and this theology has driven them to become master astronomers who've mapped Karesh's surface in meticulous detail and developed primitive rocket programs in desperate attempts to reach it. Traders can acquire advanced excavation technology, reliable chemical explosives, astronomical data, heat-storage materials, rare gemstones, and early steam engine designs, though the Asharen will pay almost any price for help achieving their civilization's defining dream: spelljamming technology to cross the void to Karesh.
