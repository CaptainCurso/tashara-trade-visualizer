# Ember-Heart

Owner: Nicholas McDowell

Ember-Heart is a scorched world of extremes, a Mercury-like planet locked in tight orbit around the binary suns where daytime temperatures soar above 800°F and the surface is a hellscape of obsidian plains, rivers of molten metal, and towering basaltic formations erupting with superheated gas geysers. The planet's thin but breathable atmosphere is laden with metallic vapors and sulfurous compounds that shimmer in the relentless amber light, while volcanic activity reshapes the landscape continuously through geothermal explosions and lava flows that crystallize into forests of mineral spires. No intelligent life has ever evolved here, but the planet hosts remarkable extremophile ecosystems—crystalline serpents that swim through solid rock, heat-drinking bioluminescent fungi, and vast swarms of metallic insects that harvest minerals from the toxic air. Beneath the obsidian crust lie the most tantalizing mysteries: massive geometric structures from an ancient precursor civilization, their unknown ceramic material resisting all divination magic, and chambers filled with crystallized data-storage devices that still glow with residual magical energy after millennia. The liquid metal rivers flowing across Ember-Heart's surface contain rare transition metals in high concentrations—osmium, iridium, and the mysterious "sun-silver," a naturally occurring magical conductor—making the planet a potentially lucrative but deadly resource extraction target for any crew brave or foolish enough to risk its wrath.
