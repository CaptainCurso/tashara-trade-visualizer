# Phorvaire

Owner: Nicholas McDowell

Phorvaire is a harsh volcanic world of constant geological upheaval, its surface dominated by obsidian plains, rivers of molten lava, toxic gas vents, and unstable tectonic plates that generate earthquakes so frequent its inhabitants barely notice them. The planet's thin but technically breathable atmosphere is laden with sulfurous compounds and metallic vapors that make prolonged exposure painful for carbon-based life, yet it hosts one of the Tashara Sphere's most remarkable civilizations: the **Khor**, a species of silicon-based crystalline beings whose translucent bodies range from quartz to obsidian and can survive intense heat, toxic atmospheres, and even brief vacuum exposure. The Khor live in vast underground cities like Khorvanum, carved through solid rock and housing over 100,000 inhabitants, their society organized around resonance-groups based on shared crystalline frequencies that allow them to literally feel each other's emotions and make decisions through harmonic consensus. Their culture values radical honesty—lying is nearly impossible within one's resonance-group—and they've developed sophisticated crystal-resonance technology including the Resonance Network, a planet-wide instant communication system using tuned crystals that could revolutionize spelljamming communications if adapted properly. Traders can acquire tuned crystals for navigation and communication, geological surveying expertise, heat-resistant materials, and stunning crystal art, though dangers include the planet's relentless volcanic activity, crystalline predators that burrow through solid rock, and hostility from traditionalist Deep Resonance Khor who view alien contact as corrupting.
