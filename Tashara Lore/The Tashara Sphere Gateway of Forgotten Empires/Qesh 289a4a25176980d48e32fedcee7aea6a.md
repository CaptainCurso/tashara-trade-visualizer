# Qesh

Owner: Nicholas McDowell

Qesh is a world of endless wind, its surface dominated by vast shallow seas and archipelagos of floating pumice islands that drift on powerful atmospheric currents circumnavigating the globe at hundreds of miles per hour. The planet's thick, moisture-laden atmosphere moves in predictable but fierce patterns called wind rivers—massive streams of air that the amphibious Qeshi people have learned to navigate with their magnificent floating cities of woven kelp-fiber and lightweight volcanic stone. These mobile settlements, the largest being Zh'lani with its fifty thousand inhabitants, are essentially massive ships guided by sail-tenders who adjust enormous membrane sails to ride the global wind currents, completing full planetary circumnavigations every thirteen months. The Qeshi themselves—slender amphibious beings evolved from newt-like ancestors with membranous gliding wings and bioluminescent skin patterns for communication—have built a sophisticated pre-industrial civilization centered on aerodynamic mastery and wind-reading, worshipping the Breath of the Twin and creating wind-songs that carry across vast distances. However, their world is not without dangers: massive six-winged aerial predators called void-maws hunt in packs through the wind rivers, pumice islands occasionally collide during storms creating deadly debris fields, and political tensions simmer between the dominant Confederation of Zh'lani and the resistant Free Current Alliance over control of the most profitable wind routes.
