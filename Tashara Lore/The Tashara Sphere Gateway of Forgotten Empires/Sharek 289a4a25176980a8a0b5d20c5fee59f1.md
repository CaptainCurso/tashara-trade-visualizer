# Sharek

Owner: Nicholas McDowell

Sharek is a lush tropical world of vast oceans, archipelagos, and a single large jungle-covered continent, blessed with warm humid climates, frequent rain, and minimal axial tilt that creates consistent year-round conditions. The planet hosts two intelligent species: the **Sharek**, sleek aquatic mammals roughly five feet long resembling otters with webbed hands and feet, dense water-repellent fur, and the ability to hold their breath for thirty minutes, who live in floating cities of tethered rafts and houseboats that can reconfigure as needed and house up to eighty thousand inhabitants; and the **Sauresh**, bipedal reptilian beings approximately human-sized with color-changing scales for camouflage who inhabit the jungle continent in hierarchical city-states built around slash-and-burn agriculture and extensive canal systems. The Sharek organize themselves into egalitarian pod-families of twenty to fifty individuals sharing all aspects of life, worshipping the Deep Mother goddess of the ocean depths and viewing their sister world Ashar as the land of the dead where souls go to rest—eerily mirroring the Asharen's opposite theology about Sharek. Traders can acquire sophisticated ship-building designs, advanced navigation techniques using stars and currents, rare marine resources including pearls and coral, underwater construction methods, and from the Sauresh access to rare jungle woods, camouflage dyes, agricultural innovations, and mercenary warriors, though dangers include massive Deep-Singer leviathans with sonic attacks, jungle predators, escalating war between Western Alliance Sharek and several Sauresh city-states over fishing rights, and the profound theological crisis that would erupt if either species learned their "afterlife" is actually inhabited by living beings.
