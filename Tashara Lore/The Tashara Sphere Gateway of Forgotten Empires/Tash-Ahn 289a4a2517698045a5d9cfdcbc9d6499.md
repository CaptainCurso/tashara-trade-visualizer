# Tash-Ahn

Owner: Nicholas McDowell

Tash-Ahn is a world of dramatic extremes, with a single massive continent spanning pole to pole and hosting vast deserts, towering mountain ranges, and deep ocean trenches whose climates vary from frozen tundra to scorching sands to tropical jungle. This environmental diversity has given rise to three distinct intelligent species: the **Tash-Vhen**, reptilian desert-dwellers with scaled skin and remarkable heat tolerance who have built sophisticated oasis cities around underground aquifers and developed advanced astronomy and glassmaking; the **Ahn-Karesh**, large stocky humanoids adapted for high-altitude mountain living with enlarged lungs who maintain oral history through elaborate song-cycles and are master stonemasons; and the **Vhen-Dok**, aquatic mammalian beings who live in floating coastal cities and practice advanced maritime navigation. The three species exist largely separate from one another, their contact limited to increasing trade through intermediaries, with each offering unique goods—the Tash-Vhen provide star charts and glass optics, the Ahn-Karesh produce superior metalwork and rope, and the Vhen-Dok supply marine resources and shipbuilding expertise. Dangers on Tash-Ahn include massive sand beetles in the deserts, avalanches and storms in the mountains, and ocean leviathans in the seas, while political tensions simmer between rival Tash-Vhen cities over water rights and between the three species over theological interpretations.
