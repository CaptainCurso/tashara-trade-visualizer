# Tashara-Major

Owner: Nicholas McDowell

Tashara-Major is the larger and brighter of the twin yellow suns at the heart of the Tashara Sphere, a G-type main-sequence star similar to Sol but slightly more luminous. It orbits in a slow gravitational waltz with its companion Tashara-Minor around a common center of mass, the two stars never more than a few million miles apart. This stellar proximity creates a system bathed in warm, golden-amber light that shifts subtly as the suns revolve, casting complex seasonal patterns across all seven worlds and ensuring that true darkness never fully claims the inhabited planets—only perpetual twilight during the brief hours when both suns dip low. The star's luminosity and heat dominate the inner system, making Ember-Heart a scorched hellscape while providing life-sustaining warmth to the outer worlds, and its fusion heart converts hundreds of millions of tons of hydrogen to helium each second, radiating energy that feeds the star-drakes basking in its corona.
