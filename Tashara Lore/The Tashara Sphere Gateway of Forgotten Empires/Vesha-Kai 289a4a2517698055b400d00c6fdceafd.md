# Vesha-Kai

Owner: Nicholas McDowell

Vesha-Kai is a verdant paradise world of deep forests, wide savannas, and crystalline rivers blessed with temperate to tropical climates and three moons—the Three Sisters—that create complex tidal patterns in its numerous seas. The planet is home to the Kai-Vesh, a species of sentient mantis-folk standing seven to eight feet tall with four arms, compound eyes capable of infrared vision, and chitinous exoskeletons ranging from deep green to golden amber, who have developed a sophisticated pre-industrial civilization organized around philosophical schools rather than geographic nations. Their culture centers on the Cycle of Necessary Sacrifice, a complex ethical framework around predation and death that shapes everything from their hunting practices to their architecture—massive stone and chitin-reinforced structures designed for their climbing abilities. The planet also hosts the Vesh-Kai, small tree-dwelling primates whose relationship with the larger Kai-Vesh exists in uncomfortable tension as the mantis-folk grapple with whether their former prey species should be recognized as full equals. Vesha-Kai offers traders access to chitin-forged armor, sophisticated philosophical texts, encyclopedic biological knowledge, and potential assistance with their primitive but ambitious rocket program aimed at reaching the Three Sisters, while dangers include massive pack-hunting Star-Fangs in the savannas and simmering political tensions between the various philosophical schools over questions of tradition versus progress.
